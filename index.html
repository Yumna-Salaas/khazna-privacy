Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the email address and name you provide when creating a Khazna account. When you add jewelry items, certificates, or receipts, they are uploaded and stored securely on our database.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information strictly to provide, maintain, and protect your jewelry inventory. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or inventory details with any third parties.

3. Storage and Security

All inventory data and uploaded files are stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Your jewelry photos, certificates, and receipts are stored in private storage buckets, accessible only to you via secure, temporary signed URLs.

4. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated inventory data at any time from your Profile settings within the app. Once deleted, this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date of this policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at khazna.app@gmail.com.
